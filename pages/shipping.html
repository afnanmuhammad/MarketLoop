Shipping Information

Delivery Times

We strive to deliver your orders as quickly as possible. Standard shipping usually takes:

  • Domestic: 3-5 business days
  • International: 7-14 business days

Shipping Costs

Shipping costs are calculated at checkout based on your location and the weight of your order. Free shipping is available for orders over $100.

Tracking

Once your order ships, you will receive a tracking number via email.
